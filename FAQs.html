General

What makes Active Adventure different from a standard babysitting agency?

+

We are a managed professional agency, not a registry. All our staff are either NHS Nurses, Certified Teachers, or Specialized Support Workers, providing a level of expertise and safety far beyond standard childcare.

Are you fully insured?

+

Yes. As a professional agency, we carry comprehensive public liability and professional indemnity insurance specifically tailored for high-stakes medical and educational care.

How do you vet your staff?

+

Every team member undergoes a "Gold Standard" vetting process: an Enhanced DBS check, mandatory identity verification, and a rigorous clinical/educational background check conducted by our founders (an NHS Nurse and a College Teacher).

Can I meet my caregiver before the booking?

+

Absolutely. Our "Select Your Professional" page allows you to view detailed profiles. For long-term or complex care, we also offer a 15-minute introductory video call.

Services

The Explorer

Outdoor-led, educator-driven discovery sessions built around movement, nature, and structured engagement.

+

How is The Explorer different from a typical park outing or nanny-led play?

+

The Explorer is structured and educator-led, not passive supervision. Sessions are intentionally designed around discovery, problem-solving, and developmental engagement using real-world environments.

What type of children benefit most from The Explorer?

+

The Explorer is ideal for children who benefit from movement, curiosity-led learning, and outdoor stimulation, including those who may struggle with traditional indoor routines.

Is The Explorer suitable for younger children or toddlers?

+

Yes. Activities are adapted based on age and developmental stage, ensuring safety while still encouraging exploration and sensory engagement.

How do you manage safety in outdoor environments?

+

All sessions follow structured risk assessments. Staff are trained in supervision, hazard awareness, and child safety protocols specific to outdoor environments.

What happens if my child prefers indoor activities?

+

Sessions can be adapted to include a blend of indoor and outdoor engagement depending on your child’s comfort, needs, and the environment available.

💼

The Executive

Flexible, high-trust professional childcare designed for responsive scheduling, premium households, and structured support.

+

What makes The Executive service different from standard childcare?

+

The Executive service is designed for flexibility and consistency, providing structured, professional care that adapts to demanding schedules rather than fixed childcare blocks.

Can I book The Executive service for both short and long durations?

+

Yes. It is designed to accommodate both short coverage periods and extended hours, including recurring or long-term arrangements.

Is The Executive suitable for high-pressure or last-minute needs?

+

Yes. While we recommend advance booking, the service is structured to support responsive care for professionals with unpredictable schedules.

Will the same professional be assigned for recurring bookings?

+

Where possible, we prioritise consistency to build familiarity and trust for both the child and family.

How do you maintain consistency across different staff?

+

All professionals operate under the same structured standards, communication expectations, and care frameworks defined by Active Adventure.

The Tourist

Flexible care for travelling families across hotels, temporary stays, and day-to-day city itineraries.

+

How does The Tourist service support families visiting the UK?

+

We provide flexible, location-based care that adapts to hotels, temporary stays, and travel itineraries, ensuring continuity for children in unfamiliar environments.

Can your staff accompany families on outings or day trips?

+

Yes. Professionals can act as trusted chaperones, supporting outings while maintaining structure, safety, and engagement.

How do you handle unfamiliar environments for children?

+

Our staff are trained to create familiarity quickly through routine, calm communication, and structured engagement tailored to each child.

Do you coordinate with hotel staff or security if required?

+

Yes. We can align with hotel procedures and security requirements to ensure smooth and safe service delivery.

Is The Tourist service suitable for international families?

+

Yes. It is specifically designed for families travelling to the UK who require trusted, professional childcare during their stay.

Overnight Companion

Specialised overnight support for families needing reassurance, continuity, sleep oversight, and structured care through the night.

+

What type of families typically choose Overnight Companion?

+

Overnight Companion is often chosen by families who need trusted overnight support, rest recovery, specialist reassurance, or a more structured night-time routine for their child.

Is Overnight Companion only for medical or complex cases?

+

No. While it can support medically aware environments, it is also suitable for families who want calm, professional overnight supervision and continuity of care.

Can Overnight Companion be tailored to waking-night or sleep-in support?

+

Yes. We offer different overnight structures depending on the level of supervision, responsiveness, and support required during the night.

Why does Overnight Companion sometimes require a longer booking window?

+

Overnight care requires continuity, preparation, and a safe handover structure. In some cases, especially when paired with RN or Support Worker placements, a minimum duration is required.

How do you decide which professional tier is right for overnight care?

+

The right professional is matched according to the child’s needs, the level of reassurance required, any medical or behavioural factors, and the complexity of the care environment.

Specialised & Overnight Care

What is "Medical-Grade" Overnight Care?

+

This service is led exclusively by Registered Nurses. It is designed for children with complex health needs, allergies, or those requiring clinical monitoring while parents rest.

Can you support children with neurodivergence or behavioral challenges?

+

Yes. We have a dedicated tier of Specialized Support Workers trained in behavioral management, routine building, and supporting children with ADHD, Autism, or other sensory needs.

Do you offer "Waking Night" or "Sleeping Night" care?

+

We offer both. A "Waking Night" means the professional is awake and monitoring the child all night; a "Sleeping Night" means they are on-site and available if the child wakes.

How do you handle medication administration?

+

Only our Registered Nurses or staff with verified clinical training are permitted to administer complex medications. This is documented in a formal care plan before the shift starts.

Logistics & Booking

Why is there a 4-hour minimum booking?

+

To ensure the highest quality of care and professional consistency for our staff, we require a minimum window to implement our educational or routine-based plans.

How far in advance do I need to book?

+

While we strive to accommodate "Shift-Responsive" requests, we recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to secure your preferred professional (Nurse/Teacher).

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Because we book high-level professionals who turn down other clinical or teaching shifts to be with you, we require 72 hours' notice for a full refund.

How do I receive updates during the care session?

+

We provide structured "Post-Care Communication," including scheduled photo check-ins and direct messaging through our secure agency platform.

Pricing & Value

Why are your rates higher than a local childminder?

+

You are paying for an accredited professional (Nurse/Teacher) and the security of a managed agency. This includes medical oversight, educational curriculum, and comprehensive insurance that standard sitters do not provide.

Do I pay the staff directly?

+

No. All payments are handled securely through our agency platform. This ensures your booking is protected and our staff are paid professional, fair-market wages.

Are there extra fees for travel or equipment?

+

For "The Tourist" or "The Explorer" sessions, any pre-agreed transport or entry fees are billed at cost. We also offer equipment rental (strollers/cots) for an additional daily fee.

Do you offer long-term or recurring packages?

+

Yes. We offer "Executive Packages" for recurring shift-support and "Explorer Passes" for weekly outdoor sessions at a slightly reduced inclusive rate.
