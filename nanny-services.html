Nanny Services

Personalized In-Home Child Care

Professional, experienced nannies providing consistent, loving care tailored to your child's unique needs in the comfort of your home.

What is a Nanny Service?

A nanny is a trusted caregiver who comes to your home to provide full-time or part-time childcare. Nannies develop strong relationships with your family and offer personalized care that supports your child's development, education, and well-being.

Unlike daycare centers, nanny care means one-on-one attention tailored to your child's schedule, dietary needs, activities, and learning style.

Key Benefits

  • One-on-one personalized attention
  • Flexible hours aligned with your schedule
  • Home-based care environment
  • Consistent caregiver relationship
  • Customized activities and learning
  • Meal preparation support
  • Household help (optional)
  • Flexible scheduling for emergencies

Why Choose Nanny Services?

Nannies offer the most personalized and flexible childcare option.

Building Deep Connections

Your nanny becomes part of your family, building meaningful relationships with your children and truly understanding their personality, preferences, and needs.

Flexible Care Environment

Care happens at your pace. Nannies adjust to your child's mood, interests, and developmental needs, creating a comfortable, stress-free environment.

Customized Support

From educational activities to meal planning, nannies work with you to create care that aligns with your family's values and goals.

Our Nanny Qualifications

Every Kiddicare nanny meets rigorous standards.

Experience & Training

  • Minimum 2+ years professional childcare experience
  • CPR and First Aid certification
  • Child development training
  • Safe sleep practice certification

Screening & Verification

  • Comprehensive background check
  • Reference verification
  • Child abuse registry screening
  • DMV/driving record check
  • Health screening and vaccination verification
