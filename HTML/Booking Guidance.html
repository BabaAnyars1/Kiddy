Step-by-Step Journey

How the booking process works from selection to confirmation.

Each stage has been designed to reduce friction while protecting service quality, professional fit, and family safety requirements. The process is structured to feel premium, not improvised.

01
Service Tier

Select Your Service

Families begin by selecting the service architecture that best matches their needs, schedule, and childcare context.

  • ELITE ANCHOR standard bookings start from a 4-hour minimum
  • Overnight Companion is structured differently and starts from a 12-hour minimum
  • The chosen tier shapes what options appear next in the flow
02
Professional Match

Choose Your Specialist

Clients then choose the professional category most suitable for the type of support required.

  • Registered Nurse (RN) for clinical safety and medically aware care
  • Certified Teacher / OCT for learning and structured engagement
  • Support Worker / CYW for behavioural support and routine continuity
03
Date Logic

Select Date & Time

The system enforces minimum duration logic automatically so the booking reflects the service architecture selected.

  • 4-hour minimum for standard care bookings
  • 12-hour minimum for Overnight Companion routes tied to RN or support-led care
  • Multiple-date bookings can be supported in a premium flow
04
Family Profile

Child & Medical Profile

Before confirmation, the system collects the information required to deliver safer, more tailored care.

  • Child count and booking complexity
  • Allergies, medical needs, and special support requirements
  • Hotel or private home location details where relevant
05
Secure Payment

Deposit or Payment

Payment is structured to secure commitment, improve scheduling confidence, and support premium service delivery.

  • Secure Stripe-style payment experience
  • Deposit options can be used in more flexible booking models
  • Client details and service scope are captured before final confirmation
06
Confirmation

Confirmation & Access

After successful booking and payment, the client receives confirmation and can be guided toward the next-stage experience.

  • Email or SMS confirmation
  • Dashboard or deep-link access for premium flow continuation
  • Preparation for profile completion and pre-care review
Process

A structured booking approach designed for clarity and confidence.

Each step is designed to ensure the right service selection, appropriate care duration, and a smooth, reliable experience from enquiry through to confirmation.

Minimum Duration Rules

ELITE ANCHOR standard bookings should hold a 4-hour minimum. Overnight Companion should enforce a 12-hour minimum when the booking is tied to RN or support-led overnight care.

Minimum booking durations ensure consistent care, appropriate staffing, and a high-quality experience for every family.

Booking Complexity Checks

The form should require the number of children. Where bookings exceed 3 children, the system should show a visible notice that a second staff member is required and that direct founder-led contact may be needed.

For larger or more complex bookings, additional support may be required to maintain safety, coverage, and service quality.
