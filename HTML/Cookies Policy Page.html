Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how ELITE ANCHOR uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, understand usage, and support service functionality.

Last Updated 2026 · United Kingdom

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide information about user interactions.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the website operates effectively, improve performance, and enhance user experience.

  • Maintain session functionality
  • Understand how users interact with pages
  • Improve booking and navigation experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality such as booking and navigation.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how the site is used.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences and settings.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how the website functions.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services used to support functionality such as analytics or secure payments.

6. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Continued use of the website indicates acceptance of the latest version.
