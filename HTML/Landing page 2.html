The Professional Tier System

We do not deploy generalists. We match the right professional tier to the right family need, so your childcare is aligned to the level of safety, structure, and specialist capability your household actually requires.

Clinical Tier

Built from a Six-Year Search for Peace of Mind

Active Adventure did not begin as a business idea. It began as a personal problem. As parents to four young boys, and as a College Teacher and NHS Registered Nurse, the founders spent years searching for childcare that matched the standards they lived by professionally.

They did not need someone to simply watch their children. They needed someone they could trust with their world — someone who understood child development, medical safety, reliable routine, and the realities of modern family pressure.

So they built the service they could not find: a managed professional agency where every person, process, and protocol is measured against one question — would we trust this person with our own four boys?

“Because the people who take care of your world should be experts in theirs.”

How the Experience Works

The premium feel comes from how the entire system is run — from matching and risk review to live visibility and structured handover.

Step 01

Needs Match

Your family is matched to the right professional tier based on medical, developmental, routine, travel, or scheduling needs.

Step 02

Pre-Shift Governance

Risk planning, medical notes, household routines, and session requirements are reviewed before care begins.

Step 03

Live Managed Care

Parents benefit from structured updates, active supervision standards, and service delivery that reflects agency governance rather than informal childcare.

Step 04

Digital Handover

Every shift closes with a documented handover covering what mattered: routines, learning, sleep, nutrition, care notes, and next-step visibility.
