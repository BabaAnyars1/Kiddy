Important Pricing Notes

Commercial rules that shape final review.

These notes help explain why the final figure may differ from a simple hourly calculation. They are also useful for setting expectations before a booking conversation begins.

Overnight Minimum Rule

If RN or Support Worker is selected for Overnight Companion, the booking must meet a minimum of 12 hours.

4+ Children Rule

Bookings for more than 3 children move to a custom quote pathway and require founder review because a second staff member may be needed.

Location Complexity

Hotel and travel-based bookings may increase price because of additional operational coordination and environment complexity.

Tailored Review

All pricing remains subject to final review based on duration, timing, specialist match, and the nature of the care environment.
