Prepare for Care

Helpful details to have ready.

Having these details prepared helps us confirm your booking smoothly and ensure the professional assigned has the information needed to deliver safe, confident support.

🧒

Child details

Names, ages, routines, allergies, medical needs, behavioural notes, and comfort preferences.

📍

Location access

Home access, hotel room details, concierge/security contact, parking, and arrival instructions.

☎

Emergency contacts

Parent/guardian contacts, authorised adults, and any relevant care or medical contacts.

For hotel-based bookings, please ensure room number, hotel security contact, and concierge or front desk details are available before care begins.
