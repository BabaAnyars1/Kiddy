About Us

Two Professionals. Four Sons. One Mission: Professionalising Peace of Mind.

Active Adventure Home Childcare was built by parents and professionals who wanted more than ordinary childcare. We created a care model grounded in safety, education, trust, and the realities of family life.

Our Story

Every business has a why. Ours was shaped by years of searching for childcare that truly matched our standards. As a College Teacher and an NHS Registered Nurse, our professional lives have always been built on curriculum, clinical safety, and accountability. At home, as parents to four young boys, we experienced the challenge of finding care that felt both dependable and deeply aligned with what children actually need.

We were not looking for someone to simply supervise. We were looking for people we could trust with our whole world — people who understood child development, medical safety, and the value of a stable, nurturing routine.

Why We Built Active Adventure

We created the service we could not find ourselves: premium home childcare that combines warmth with professional standards, and flexibility with genuine peace of mind.

Everything we offer begins with the same question: would we trust this person and this system with our own children?

The Parent-Professional Perspective

Active Adventure was born in the quiet, honest moments of parenting — when it became clear that many families were being forced to choose between rigid nursery structures and informal, lightly vetted childcare. We decided to build a third option: a managed professional agency designed around what modern families actually need.

We Are the Client

We evaluate every staff member against the highest standard possible: would we feel completely confident leaving our own four boys in their care? That standard shapes every hiring decision.

We Are the Experts

Our approach combines NHS clinical governance with educational pedagogy, creating a childcare model that is structured, thoughtful, and intentionally high quality.

We Built the Third Option

Families deserve more than a choice between convenience and trust. Our model delivers flexibility without compromising on oversight, safety, or child development.

Our Triple-Accredited Pedigree

We do not simply hire staff. We curate a team of professionals whose training, judgement, and specialist experience allow us to deliver a higher standard of in-home childcare.

The Clinical Tier

NHS Nurses who bring hospital-grade safety awareness, medical confidence, allergy management understanding, and calm oversight into the home environment.

The Educational Tier

Certified Teachers who transform daily care into meaningful learning moments, using structured development and engaging experiences to support each child.

The Behavioral Tier

Specialised Support Workers with experience in routine building, neurodivergence support, and household stability during demanding schedules and transitions.

Our Promise to You

We understand what it feels like to keep searching because “good enough” does not feel safe enough. We understand the pressure of long shifts, unpredictable schedules, and the emotional weight of leaving your children in someone else’s care.

Active Adventure is the solution we built for ourselves, and now proudly offer to other families who want more than a service. They want certainty, professionalism, and care they can genuinely rely on.

For Local Professionals

Reliable, structured care that supports demanding routines and changing schedules.

For Executives & High-Performing Families

A premium standard of oversight, flexibility, and confidence when time and trust both matter.

For Visiting Families

Professional in-home childcare in the UK that allows you to settle, work, or travel with peace of mind.

Because the people who care for your world should be experts in theirs.
