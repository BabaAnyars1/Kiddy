Why Parents Trust Active Adventure

The 14 Pillars of Clinical & Pedagogical Excellence

I. Clinical & Professional Credentials

1. NHS Registered Nurses (RN)

Practitioners with hospital-grade experience, ensuring clinical safety in the home.

2. Certified Teachers (QTS/PGCE)

Academic leads who transform childcare into active pedagogical discovery.

3. First Aid/CPR Certified

Mandatory certification for all staff, refreshed via our clinical lead audits.

4. Complex Needs Support

Specialized care for high-acuity allergies, neurodiversity, and medical conditions.

II. Vetting & Governance

5. Enhanced DBS Background Checks

Comprehensive multi-stage security screening and continuous monitoring.

6. Agency-Managed Architecture

Not a marketplace; we take full accountability for governance and staff performance.

7. Fully Insured

Multi-million pound professional indemnity and medical malpractice coverage.

8. Vetted In-Home Protocol

Strict security and safety audits performed before any practitioner enters your residence.
