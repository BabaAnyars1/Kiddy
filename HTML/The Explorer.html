The Explorer

Nature-Based Discovery & Forest Pedagogy

Active Engagement. STEM Discovery. All-Weather Resilience.

In a world of screens and structured indoor play, The Explorer is our antidote. This is not a simple walk in the park — it is a teacher-led outdoor curriculum that turns the natural world into a high-level classroom.

Explore the Curriculum View Standards
Teacher-led outdoor learning

A Premium Outdoor Learning Experience

Led exclusively by Certified Teachers (OCT/PGCE) and Forest School Educators, The Explorer earns its premium place by replacing passive childminding with active, inquiry-based learning. Every session is designed with educational intent, movement, and resilience at its core.

Children do not just spend time outside — they investigate, build, identify, question, problem-solve, and connect with the natural world in ways that support long-term confidence and development.

“Childhood is an adventure that should not be confined to four walls. We provide the experts to lead the way.”

Beyond the Playground: Our Outdoor Curriculum

We do not wait for the perfect day. We believe resilience is built in the rain and curiosity is sparked in the wild. The Explorer is structured around four core pillars of development.

STEM Inquiry & Discovery

We use the local environment to teach biology, physics, and ecology. From native flora to self-built shelters, children learn through real-time investigation and hands-on problem solving.

Physical Risk Competence

Under expert supervision, children learn to climb, balance, assess terrain, and build healthy confidence in controlled environments that support safe, adventurous play.

Native Wildlife Identification

Each session can become a tracking mission, helping children identify birds, insects, and mammals while building a life-long connection to the UK’s natural heritage.

Environmental Stewardship

We teach Leave No Trace principles, helping children understand respect, responsibility, and their role in protecting the places they explore.

The Teacher Advantage: Why It Matters

The difference between a sitter and a Certified Teacher is the intent behind the play. Our staff bring professional judgement, pedagogical structure, and dynamic outdoor safety expertise into every Explorer session.

Guided Inquiry

Our teachers do not simply provide answers. They ask the questions that spark critical thinking, observation, and curiosity-led discovery.

Safety & Dynamic Risk Assessment

Every session begins with a professional safety sweep and dynamic risk assessment so that wild play remains safe play, even in changing outdoor conditions.

Accredited Expertise

Booking The Explorer gives families access to the same calibre of pedagogical expertise found in elite private forest schools, offered flexibly in 1:1 or small-group formats.

Operational Logistics

Designed for real family schedules, The Explorer combines educational depth with practical flexibility and consistent communication.

All-Weather Preparedness

Rain, shine, or snow — the mission continues. Parents receive a practical kit list so children arrive equipped for the British elements.

Flexible Adventure Windows

Available as short-burst 4-hour sessions or full-day Expedition bookings, giving families room to choose what suits their routine best.

The Digital Trail

Parents receive high-quality photo updates and a Discovery Log showing what their child learned, found, explored, and achieved during the session.

The Explorer Standards

This service is premium because its standards are uncompromising. Every session is supported by accredited leadership, full agency vetting, and managed insurance coverage.

Accredited Leaders Only

Every session is led by a Certified Teacher (OCT/PGCE) or a Level 3 Forest School Practitioner.

Full Agency Vetting

Enhanced DBS, Pediatric First Aid, and specialised outdoor safety certifications are mandatory for all Explorer staff.

Insured Discovery

Our managed agency insurance covers off-site and outdoor activities, providing reassurance that private sitters simply cannot replicate.
