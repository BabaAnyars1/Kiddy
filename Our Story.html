Our Story

Born from a 6-Year Search for Peace of Mind

For parents who need more than childcare — they need genuine trust.

To our fellow parents and visitors, if you are reading this, you likely know the feeling. It is that quiet, persistent weight of responsibility that never quite leaves your shoulders. For our family, that weight was a constant companion for six years.

As parents to four energetic boys, our home has always been full of life, laughter, and a fair share of chaos. But one thing remained missing: time for ourselves, and care we could trust with our world.

Why We Started How We Built It
Built by parents, shaped by professional standards

The Professional Gap

Our standards were high because our professional lives demanded it. As a College Teacher and an NHS Registered Nurse, we were not looking for a babysitter. We were looking for someone who shared our level of judgement, professionalism, and care.

Child Development

We wanted a professional who understood development, inquiry, and meaningful engagement in the way a teacher does.

Clinical Confidence

We needed a clinician who could respond to emergencies with the same calm clarity we would expect from an NHS nurse.

Real Household Support

We wanted support for the little big things, like the school run, and the big big things, like finally getting a full night of sleep.

ELITE ANCHOR: The Professional Solution

ELITE ANCHOR was not born in a boardroom. It was born at 3:00 AM in our living room, when we decided to build the service we could not find for our own family.

Clinical Standards

We brought NHS medical standards into overnight and complex childcare so safety is built into the service from the ground up.

Educational Expertise

We built a model where teachers turn ordinary time into Explorer-style, nature-based, inquiry-led learning.

Routine Stability

We included support workers who can provide the behavioural consistency and practical structure busy households rely on.

Our Promise to You

When you book with us, you are not just hiring a staff member. You are benefiting from a vetting process designed by an NHS Nurse and a College Teacher who built this service around what they would demand for their own children.

Before anyone joins our team, we ask one question: Would we trust this person with our four boys?

If the answer is not a resounding yes, they do not wear our uniform.

For Local Families

We created ELITE ANCHOR so you do not have to wait six years for a trusted night off or a few hours of reliable support.

For Visitors

Whether you are visiting the city or balancing a demanding schedule, we provide elite support that is structured, safe, and genuinely dependable.

Welcome to the family.

Because the people who take care of your world should be experts in theirs.
