Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

How do I get started with booking a service?

Simply visit our Booking page, select the service type that fits your needs, choose your preferred dates and times, and submit your request. Our team will match you with the perfect caregiver and confirm within 24 hours.

Are your caregivers background-checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every caregiver undergoes comprehensive background screening including criminal checks, reference verification, and child abuse registry screening before joining our network.

What if I need last-minute care?

We offer same-day booking options for many services. Contact our support team at hello@kiddicare.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567 for emergency care availability.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Yes, cancellations and rescheduling are available up to 24 hours before your scheduled service. Please contact us as soon as possible for changes.

What safety measures are in place?

We maintain industry-leading safety standards including caregiver training, real-time communication with parents, incident reporting protocols, and comprehensive liability insurance.

How much does Kiddicare cost?

Pricing varies by service type and location. Contact our team for a personalized quote based on your specific needs and scheduling requirements.
