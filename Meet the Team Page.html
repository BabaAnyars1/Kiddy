Founders

The dual-lead vision shaping every placement.

Our leadership combines healthcare, education, and a premium service mindset. This dual-lead model is one of the defining differentiators of ELITE ANCHOR — helping us support families with both emotional intelligence and professional rigour.

Founder & Clinical Lead

Clinical Safety, Regulation & Family Reassurance

NHS Registered Nurse Leadership

Our clinical leadership ensures that safety is not treated as a marketing claim, but as an operational discipline. This perspective supports our overnight services, travel care, complex family requirements, and premium reassurance model.

  • Guides safeguarding, safety standards, and escalation pathways.
  • Brings confidence to families requiring medically aware support.
  • Supports calm, structured decision-making for complex cases.
Founder & Education Lead

Development, Engagement & Structured Care Design

Qualified Teaching & Curriculum Perspective

Our educational leadership shapes the way we think about children’s time, attention, curiosity, and developmental rhythm. This is why our care style goes beyond supervision and into active, meaningful engagement.

  • Guides activity structure, routine design, and developmental quality.
  • Ensures care remains engaging, intentional, and age-considered.
  • Supports service design across Explorer, Executive, and Tourist care models.
Specialist Team

Our professional care tiers.

Our wider team is organised through a clearly defined tiering system, allowing families to understand the calibre and suitability of the professional supporting them. Each tier plays a different role within our care architecture, while still operating inside the same premium standards framework.

NHS RN Specialist

Clinical Tier

Our Registered Nurse specialists are best suited to overnight cases, medically aware environments, travel-related complexity, and situations where parents value an elevated level of clinical reassurance.

Qualified Teacher (QTS)

Educational Tier

Our teacher-led professionals support children through learning-minded engagement, outdoor structure, curiosity-led activities, routine support, and thoughtful developmental pacing.

Specialised Support Worker

Behavioural & Routine Tier

Our support specialists bring consistency, practical family assistance, behavioural awareness, and reliable routine continuity for families requiring structured and adaptable support.
