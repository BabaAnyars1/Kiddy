What is a Babysitter?

A babysitter provides short-term, flexible childcare for evenings, weekends, or when you need backup care. Perfect for date nights, work commitments, or personal time.

Our babysitters are vetted, experienced caregivers trained in child safety and engagement. They keep your children entertained, safe, and happy while you're away.

  • Same-day booking options
  • Perfect for date nights

  • Competitive hourly rates

  • No long-term commitment
