The Executive

High-Performance Support for High-Pressure Schedules

Precision Logistics. Reliable Flexibility. Professional Accountability.

As a professional, your schedule does not always fit a 9-to-5 box. Whether you are navigating unpredictable shifts, late boardroom sessions, or early commutes, The Executive is designed to deliver seamless, elite-level childcare that moves at the pace of your career.

Explore Booking Options Why This Tier Matters
Managed care for demanding schedules

A Managed Service, Not Gap-Filling

The Executive is not designed as stopgap childcare. It is a managed care architecture led by Specialised Support Workers (CYW) and Certified Teachers, ensuring your children maintain routine, structure, and meaningful engagement while you stay focused on your professional responsibilities.

This service is built for households where reliability is non-negotiable and flexibility must be matched by genuine professional accountability.

“Your career should not be a barrier to elite childcare, and your childcare should not be a barrier to your career. We provide the professional bridge.”

Flexible Booking: Short & Long Hours

We have removed the rigidity of traditional nurseries and the unreliability of private sitters. The Executive adapts to your operational needs with structured care windows designed around real professional routines.

The Early Start

5:00 AM – 9:00 AM

Ideal for commuters and healthcare professionals. We manage the morning transition, breakfast, and the school run so you can leave calm, focused, and on time.

The Extended Shift

5:00 PM – 10:00 PM+

For evenings when work runs over. We manage dinner, homework support, and bedtime wind-down so your children remain settled and safe.

The Double-Shift Long Haul

For 12-hour rotations

Designed for intense project weeks and long professional rotations, bridging the gap between school, evening routine, and sleep with consistent high-standard care.

Why the Executive Tier Is Different

Standard childcare is often passive. The Executive is an active, managed service built to support the efficiency of your household, not just occupy time.

Routine Maintenance

Our Specialised Support Workers do more than supervise. They execute your household routine with care, from behavioural support plans to extracurricular preparation.

Educational Enrichment

When led by our Certified Teachers, after-school hours become a meaningful window for homework support, STEM inquiry, and curriculum-aligned play.

The Agency Standard of Reliability

Because we are a managed agency, your family is not dependent on one individual’s availability. We provide a vetted professional team and a real backup plan when schedules change.

Professional Logistics & Safety

The Executive is designed for serious routines and high-pressure homes, with systems that prioritise consistency, communication, and professional standards.

Minimum 4-Hour Booking

This gives your professional caregiver enough time to implement a high-standard routine and provide meaningful engagement, not rushed supervision.

Real-Time Logistics

Use our secure agency platform for handover notes, so you know exactly how the afternoon or evening went before you even walk through the door.

Vetted & Accredited

Every Executive caregiver is an accredited professional with an Enhanced DBS and verified experience working in high-pressure environments.
