Daycare Services

Structured Learning & Play Environment

Quality daycare programs with educational activities, healthy meals, and nurturing supervision where children learn, play, and grow.

What is Daycare?

Daycare provides structured, supervised care for young children in a group setting. Our daycare programs combine play-based learning with social development, preparing children for school while providing reliable, affordable care for working parents.

From infants through preschool, our centers offer age-appropriate activities, healthy meals, and a safe, nurturing environment where every child thrives.

Key Benefits

  • Structured daily schedules
  • Educational programming
  • Social interaction with peers
  • Nutritious meals included
  • Screen time policies
  • Physical activity & play
  • School readiness preparation
  • Affordable enrollment options

Why Choose Our Daycare?

Programs by Age Group

Age-appropriate care and learning for every stage.

Infant Care (6 weeks - 12 months)

Loving, attentive care for infants with emphasis on bonding, sensory development, and establishing healthy routines.

  • Small group sizes (max 4:1 ratio)
  • Bottle feeding & nap support
  • Diaper changing & hygiene
  • Early sensory activities
  • Daily progress reports for parents

Toddlers (1 - 2 years)

Interactive learning with activities focused on language, motor skills, and social development in a safe, secure environment.

  • 6:1 caregiver ratio
  • Language development activities
  • Potty training support
  • Music & movement
  • Story time & play

Preschool (3 - 4 years)

Pre-academic learning with structured activities building coordination, literacy, and social skills for school readiness.

  • 10:1 caregiver ratio
  • Letter & number learning
  • Art & creative projects
  • Science exploration
  • Group activities & games

Pre-K (4 - 5 years)

School preparation with academics, structured learning, and activities building confidence and independence for kindergarten.

  • 12:1 caregiver ratio
  • Reading & writing readiness
  • Math concepts
  • Social-emotional learning
  • Kindergarten transition prep
